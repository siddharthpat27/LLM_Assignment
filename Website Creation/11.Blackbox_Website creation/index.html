iPhone

The iPhone is a line of smartphones designed and marketed by Apple Inc. It runs Apple's iOS mobile operating system, known for its user-friendly interface and extensive app ecosystem.

Features:

  • Face ID or Touch ID for secure authentication
  • High-quality cameras for photos and videos
  • Powerful performance with Apple-designed chips

MacBook

The MacBook is a line of laptops designed and manufactured by Apple Inc. It offers a balance of portability and performance, making it suitable for a wide range of tasks.

Features:

  • Retina display for crisp visuals
  • Long-lasting battery life
  • Thin and lightweight design
